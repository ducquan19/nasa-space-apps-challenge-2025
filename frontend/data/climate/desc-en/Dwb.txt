The climate is humid continental with hot or warm summers influenced by monsoons and characterized by sharp and extreme seasonal variations. Winters are very cold, with the coldest month averaging below 0°C. Summers are hot, with at least one month averaging above 22°C, and at least four months averaging above 10°C. Seasonal rainfall is highly variable, with the tropical monsoon bringing heavy summer rainfall. Rainfall in the wettest month of summer is often at least 10 times that of the driest month of winter.
